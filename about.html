Software that respects your PC

PCSoftware builds focused, honest software for Windows — and, over time, for the platforms our users actually work on.

Our mission

PC optimization without the scare tactics

Most "PC cleaner" software in this category survives on fear: fake scan results, exaggerated "junk" counts, and popups designed to pressure a purchase. We think that's a broken way to build trust with the people using your software.

PCSoftware exists to prove the opposite is possible — tools built entirely on official, documented Windows APIs, that report only what's real, and that never touch anything critical without your explicit confirmation.
